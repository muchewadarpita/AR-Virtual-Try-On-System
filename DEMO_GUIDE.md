Before recording:

- [ ] Good lighting (face the light)
- [ ] Plain background
- [ ] Camera at eye level
- [ ] Full upper body visible
- [ ] Stable internet connection

## üåü Pro Tips

6. **Show Value**: Focus on what makes it cool
7. **Test Playback**: Watch your recording before submitting
